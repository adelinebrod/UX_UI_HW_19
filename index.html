Adeline Rodriguez

¿Qué tal? I'm Adeline. I am a UX/UI Designer based in Los Angeles. My background sits at the intersection of social science research, hospitality experience, and storytelling. My designs strive to create delightful and intuitive user experiences that overcome a myriad of usability and business obstacles.

My UX Skills

User Empathy

Empathizing with users entails translating user pain points and needs discovered in research into actionable solutions. It also entails anticipating user needs throughout the design process.

Prototyping

I strive to make my prototypes seamless and simple. A majority of my projects focus on task completion, which makes simple and functional interactions paramount to the user experience.

Design Evaluation

Design analysis is a pivotal step in the redesign process. I critically think about the text hierarchy, color palette, imagery, and other elements to understand what they do for a digital product and to analyze the potential problems they may create.

Projects

Maco Mobile App

Novice travelers needed a way to feel more confident and prepared for their journeys ahead. Maco solves this problem by encouraging community engagement, offering downloadable guides, and extended filters to ensure users relished in the moment.

Tinklr Mobile App

Tinklr offers a functional and delightful solution for the eternal human quest to find a suitable public restroom. Tinklr was created for parents, those with special needs, those who simply enjoy a clean restroom, and anyone in between.
